Sterilization

Sterilization was one of the main points in the chrysalids. The waknuk society believed in making their society pure, having no imperfections, and if it was found that someone was abnormal they would often be sterilized and sent out to the fringes.

Genetic/Traits

A huge portion of eugenics throughout history, if not all, has been because of how someone is shaped in terms of traits and their genetics. Josef Mengele was part of the disaster in the Holocaust, he was a "doctor" that tested on humans; whether the test was to make the eyes a different color or change something in their body (surgery without anesthesia), he was constantly trying to purify and help with Hitler's belief.

Society/Race
